Helliwell Park, Hornby Island
Oil Painting on Canvas
24"x30"
Private Collection

Helliwell Park is a parkland of unsurpassed beauty on Hornby Island. On a hot summer day the orange grass fairly glows and the heat
makes the park feel as though it is on fire. The flow of this painting reflects this imagery. I originally painted this scene in a more realistic way that lacked colour. I decided to simplify forms and colours to convey the essence of Helliwell on a hot day.
