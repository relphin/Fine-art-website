Reaching Out
Oil Painting on Canvas
11"x14"
Studio 737

Trees on the west coast of Vancouver Island are shaped by the wild conditions that exist there. These trees can be found along the Wild Pacific Trail near Ucluelet. They can take on almost human attributes in their struggle against the elements.
