Crocuses in my Garden
Oil Painting on Canvas
8"x10"
Private Collection

Since moving to the west coast, the garden in our backyard is a constant source of beauty and enjoyment. The crocuses which come out in early spring are the harbingers of the incredible variety of flowering shrubs, trees and plants which follow. I find the patterns which the groups of crocuses form fascinating and I always enjoy painting them.
