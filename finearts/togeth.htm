Together
Oil Painting on Canvas
36"x24"
Private Art Collection

This is an image derived from two trees, isolated and desolate on the north end of Vancouver Island in Port Hardy. Trees have been scoured by wind and water in ways that provide a metaphor for the way in which people have been shaped by equivalent influences.
