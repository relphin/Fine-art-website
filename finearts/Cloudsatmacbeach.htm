Clouds at Mackenzie Beach, Tofino
Oil Painting on Canvas
8"x10"
Private Collection
MacKenzie Beach is a place where my family and I often stay when we visit the west coast of Vancouver Island. This painting is meant to both unsettle and settle one’s feelings as it is viewed. Photos seldom capture the intrinsic feeling of a location. The reality captured in a photograph is merely a shadow of that which truly exists. Art is about capturing that true reality and creating something which can awaken the observer.
