Broken Group Islands, Ucluelet
24"x30"
Oil on Canvas
Private Collection
My family walked the Wild Pacific Trail in Ucluelet on an incredible day. The Broken Island group was clearly visible and the true sense of the west coast was evident. This painting was an attempt to capture that mood and beauty.
