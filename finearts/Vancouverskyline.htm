Vancouver Skyline
 Oil Painting on Canvas
30"x40"
A photograph of the Vancouver core usually produces an image which fails to capture the city's vitality. A painting can be executed with the goal of reproducing the actual state of a place which is more than the shadow produced in a photograph.
