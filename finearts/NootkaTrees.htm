Yuquot Life
Oil
16"x20"
Private Collection

Yuquot means "Where the Winds Blow" and is a first nation settlement in Nootka Sound off Vancouver Island. The lovely trees and seastacks which brave the elements here provide a metaphor for the lives of people buffeted by the winds of change.
       Friendly Cove is a wonderful vacation spot on Nootka Island west of Gold River. To get there, the Uchuck III working freighter travels Nootka Sound and Luna the killer whale tags along. At the cove is a beach of marble like rocks which whales love to use to scratch their backs. Primitive cabins perch on the edge of a lake while out the back window lies the ocean. One of the few remaining manned lighthouses exists here.
