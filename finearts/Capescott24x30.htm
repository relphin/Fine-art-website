Rocks at San Josef Bay, Cape Scott, 
24"x30"
Oil on Canvas
Private Collection
The northwest coast of Vancouver Island is an incredible place to experience the shaping influences of the Pacific Ocean. An hour walk takes you to San Josef Bay, a secluded beach with weather beaten trees. Here the full force of the Pacific weather systems can be felt through the shapes of the trees and the rock formations. In this painting the link between the elements is captured through the movement found in the swirling sky and weathered, twisted trees.
