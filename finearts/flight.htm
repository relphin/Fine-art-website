Flight
24"x30"
Oil
The painting of more representational pieces led me to realize that the true joy in producing  a painting comes not from the accurate copying of an object but rather from the letting go and producing what is actually there. Rather than imposing my emotional state on nature I prefer the art of  "actualism" whereby the actual nature of a scene is depicted. 
      West coast skies are an incredible sight with their fascinating patterns and colour. Patterns in the sky reflect patterns which exist in our everyday lives which are hidden to most. My art attempts to capture the designs and ideas embedded in nature.
