 Wickaninnish Beach, Pacific Rim National Park
 Oil Painting on Canvas
20"x16"
Private collection 

The west coast of Vancouver Island is exposed to extremely wild weather and storms. Everything about that coast conveys a sense of nature in its extreme. Motion is everywhere but is seldom captured by a still picture. Trees, sky and water are in constant movement and on a stormy day there is a vibrant energy that is impossible to capture with a dull colour palette.
