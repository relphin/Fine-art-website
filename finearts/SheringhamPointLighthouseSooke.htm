Sheringham Point Lighthouse, Sooke
20"x16"
oil on canvas
Private Collection

This lighthouse is located at the south tip of Vancouver Island near Sooke. The passage of time has made manned lighthouses somewhat anachronistic. These structures stand against the elements warning ships of the perils of the west coast. This painting illustrates the fragile nature of manmade structures in the face of west coast nature.
