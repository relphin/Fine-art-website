Ferry at Horseshoe Bay, Vancouver
Oil Painting on Canvas
24"x48"

I finished and hung this painting the day this ferry crashed into the docks at Horseshoe Bay. This was an exciting painting for me because of the breakthrough in the method that I used. You can see the impact marks from my brush in the trees on the lower left corner of the painting. At the end of this painting I was drained and exhausted. It felt wonderful.
