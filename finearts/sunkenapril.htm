Sunken Gardens, April, Butchart Gardens
Oil Painting on Canvas
20"x16"
Private Collection
The sunken Butchart Gardens in the springtime has a beauty that conveys to me in a single place much of the floral loveliness that is found on Vancouver Island. It is a delight to view and to paint.
