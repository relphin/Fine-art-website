April at the Butchart Gardens
Oil Painting on Canvas
30"x40"
The Butchart Sunken Gardens were once a rock quarry located north of Victoria, Canada in the Saanich Peninsula. These gardens convey the floral loveliness that is found on Vancouver Island in the springtime. It is a delight to view and to paint. The multitude of flowers here requires one to neglect the individual to see the pattern of the whole garden. The senses ignore the details and focus on the patterns that overwhelm the spectator of these incredible gardens.
