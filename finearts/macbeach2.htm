Mackenzie Beach II, Tofino
Oil Painting on Canvas
16"x20"
Private Collection

This painting is meant to both unsettle and settle one’s feelings as it is viewed. The heavily textured sky forms a living link from the bright setting sun to the trees that rise up from the land. The red and green complementary colours that dominate the foreground convey the reflective emotions evoked while walking the beaches of the Pacific Northwest.
