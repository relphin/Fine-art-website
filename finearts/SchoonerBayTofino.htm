Schooner Bay, Tofino
Oil Painting on Canvas
20"x16"
Private Collection
Studio 737
Schooner Bay is a lovely location in Pacific Rim National Park along the west coast of Vancouver Island. Photos seldom capture the intrinsic feeling of a location. The reality captured in a photograph is merely a shadow of that which truly exists. Art is about capturing that true reality and creating something which can awaken the observer. This painting was made when I wanted to create something very quickly to try to capture the essence of a place. The trees in the background were for me the most interesting part of this work as they were painted in very rapidly and with a great deal of enjoyment.
