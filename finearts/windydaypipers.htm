Windy Day at Pipers Lagoon, Nanaimo, BC
 Oil Painting on Canvas
16"x20"
STUDIO 737
Pipers lagoon is a favourite spot. On a windy day, the sense of west coast nature is in full evidence. This painting is a celebration of that west coast feeling.
