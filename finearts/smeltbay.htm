Smelt Bay, Cortes Island
Oil Painting on Canvas
24"x30"
Private Collection
This beach is found on one of the most beautiful Gulf Island at Canada's West Coast. Instead of sand or pebbles it
has large water smoothed rocks about 6" across. These rocks scaled perfectly to the small west coast pebbles that
that have been inlaid into the beach portion of this painting. The heavy texture gives a tremendous sense of energy and movement to this piece.
