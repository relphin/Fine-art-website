Trees on a Sea Stack, Brady's Beach, Bamfield
20"x16"
Private Collection
Brady's Beach is located on the west side of Bamfield where the wild Pacific Ocean first hits  North America. The astounding sea stacks which reach up out of the ocean here make this beach one of the most special sights to be seen on Vancouver Island. Bamfield itself is a wonderful village located on two sides of an inlet connected by water taxis and small boats. Access is from Port Alberni by logging road or by a working freighter called the "Lady Rose".
