Wickaninnish Centre II, Pacific Rim National Park
Oil Painting on Canvas
8"x10"
Studio 737

Wickaninnish Centre is located in the Pacific Rim National Park on the west coast of Vancouver Island. This centre is a wonderful place to learn about the activities of the park and to experience first hand the forces of nature which shape the shoreline in the park. Its rustic design blends well into its surroundings.
