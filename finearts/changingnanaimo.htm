Changing Nanaimo
Oil Painting on Canvas
24"x30" 
Private Collection

The Vancouver Island city, Nanaimo, is in a state of rapid change as it evolves from a coal mining town to an urban tourist centre linked to Vancouver, BC via the fast ferry shown in the painting. Ferries are evolving as well as they keep up with the demand the city puts on them.
