Entrance Island Lighthouse, Nanaimo, BC 
Oil Painting on Canvas
20"x16"
Private Collection

Lighthouse up and down our west coast are becoming automated and as they do these buildings are becoming a symbol of changing times.
The wobbling lighthouse illustrates the impermanence of man's constructs against the backdrop of the massive unchanging mountains.
