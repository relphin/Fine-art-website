Spirit of the Malahat
Oil Painting on Canvas
30"x40"
Private Collection

This stunning view can be seen near the summit of the Malahat Highway just outside of Victoria B.C., Canada. Trees are altered by the fierce nature of the west coast and often take on forms resembling the living spirit of the forces so active there.
