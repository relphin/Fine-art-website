 Westwood Lake, Nanaimo, BC
Oil Painting on Canvas
20"x16"
Private Collection
One can find almost anywhere, the image of trees left standing after man made flooding has covered a valley floor. Sometimes the flooding occurred over 50 years ago and yet still these trees are left standing. They provide a analogy for the tenacity of the human spirit in the face of adversity. These trees located as they are in a flooded lake convey the tragic state of affairs so many people are in. These particular trees can be found on a hike around Westwood Lake in Nanaimo.
