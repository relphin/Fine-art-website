Church
Oil Painting on Canvas
30"x24"
Private Collection

A small church on Cortes Island near Vancouver Island was the model for this painting. The ruined state of the church is related to the wet, rainy conditions of the west coast Canadian rain forest. Buildings here cannot be painted independently of their environment.  To capture the interaction between building and nature, swirls of textured paints constructed the abstract environment surrounding the church that is seen to bend in these primal forces. The movement of the surroundings envelope the church and give it life.
