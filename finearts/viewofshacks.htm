View of Shacks
Oil Painting on Canvas
8"x10"
Private Collection

The Shack Islands are a well known landmark in north Nanaimo, BC. A short hike around Pipers Lagoon will take you to a lovely peninsula overlooking these shacks. At low tide, one can make a brief excursion over to these islands on foot.
