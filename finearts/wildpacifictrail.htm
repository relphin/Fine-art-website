Wild Pacific Trail,Ucluelet
 Fine Art Oil Painting on Canvas
24"x30" 
For Price Contact Waterlily
Gallery, Ucluelet

My family walked the Wild Pacific Trail in Ucluelet on an incredible day. There is a place on the trail where the ocean is broken up by pieces of land jutting into it. Bald eagles are everywhere. This painting was an attempt to capture the incredible mood and beauty of this wonderful location.
